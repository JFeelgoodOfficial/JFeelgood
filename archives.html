Archives wall

2004 – 2025 in one breath.

This archive is the long view of my work – a wall of paintings from the years when I was mostly just trying not to sleepwalk through my own life. These are the experiments, detours, and small breakthroughs that led to Thought Entropy, Self Work, and The Book of Shadow Work.

You’ll see shifts in color, subject, and medium as I learned to translate questions about identity, family, grief, faith, and possibility into simple visual meditations. Early pieces sit beside later ones without hierarchy; it’s less a “greatest hits” and more a record of someone learning how to see, feel, and stay present.

Take this archive as an invitation to wander. Zoom out and let the whole mosaic hit you, or pause on whatever piece your attention circles back to. The story here is about an artist evolving and finding new ways to speak.
