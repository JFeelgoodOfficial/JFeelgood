Archives wall

2004 – 2020 in one breath.

This is the long view – around 150 pieces from the years that led to Thought Entropy, Self Work, and The Book of Shadow Work. No captions, just a mosaic of seasons, experiments, and small revolutions in paint.

Welcome to the "2004-2020 Archives" at JFeelgood Art Gallery, a comprehensive collection that chronicles the artist's evolution over 16 transformative years. As a gallery committed to preserving and celebrating the creative journey, we are proud to present this archival series, which offers a deep dive into JFeelgood's artistic development. The "2004-2020 Archives" showcase a rich tapestry of works, spanning various themes, styles, and mediums. This collection is a testament to JFeelgood's commitment to experimentation and growth, featuring early explorations that laid the groundwork for the distinctive style seen in later works. From the bold strokes of the early 2000s to the refined mixed media pieces of the 2010s, this archive is a visual narrative of artistic mastery in progress. Each piece within this archive has been carefully selected to reflect the significant milestones in JFeelgood's career. As you explore this collection, you'll witness the artist's journey from emerging talent to a recognized name in contemporary art. The works within this archive are not just historical records; they are timeless expressions that continue to resonate with themes of identity, transformation, and the human experience. We invite you to explore the "2004-2020 Archives" at the JFeelgood Art Gallery, where the past meets the present, offering a unique opportunity to appreciate the full scope of the artist's creative legacy.
